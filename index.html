Our Story

We were not satisfied with current timelapse slider mounts on the market. We at MotionLapse want to make timelapse photography available to everyone.

Accessibility

  • In recent years there has been many innovations in the photography industry. Amateur photographers can do alot more and for less. One area we feel is still dominated by professionals are timelapse camera mounts due to the technical aspect of this style of photography. We want to change this.

Portability

  • It is in the nature of photographers to go explore and travel to new locations. Camera equiptment tends to be bulky, sometimes requiring professional cases for transportation. We wanted a solution that can be easily transportable.

Introducing MotionLapse

A simple yet powerful tool for timelapse photography

MotionLapse is a timelapse slider mount which can be placed anytime anywhere.

Flexible Track

The flexible track opens up many more possiblities for different panned shots. Use the flexible grip to attach onto different surfaces, mold the track to your desired shape and the cart will follow!

Easy Clip

Our simple clip design ensures that you can get up and running quickly. Simply place the cart along the track and clip. No fuss!

Tri-Wheel

The three wheel system ensures accurate and precise motion , allowing for stable and smooth shots.

Control

MotionLapse can be easily controlled using a variety of user inputs.

LCD

Easily Navigate through all the options using the built in LCD

Buttons

Navigate through the LCD options using the three buttons

BlueTooth Control

Control MotionLapse using bluetooth through our phone app. Available on both IOS and Android.

Customisable Panning

The flexible slider allows motion in all three axes. This allows the camera to follow panning paths the way you want.

Website

What Makes a Good Website?

  • - Clear value proposition and goal: by fully understand your audience this ensures better user engagement
  • - Intuitive navigation: Easy to understand navigation allows users to find content more easily
  • - Clear readable information: ensure site information is clear and consise. Sometimes less means more!
  • - Aesthetically pleasing layout: Draws and keeps users engaged in content
  • - Safety: Ensure that users feel safe. Place safety disclaimers if users need to input their own data

Accessibility & Considerations

  • Website accessibility is the consideration of website design for users with disabilities. These include conditions such a audio or visual impairment.
  • Website Considerations:
  • - Text Placement: Good constrast ensures text can be read clearly.
  • - Webpage format: Website can be viewed on other platforms such as mobile phones
  • - User Safety: No loud noise,flashing or flickering of pictures etc.
  • - Text to speech compatibility: Text can be read using a text to speech internet browser add-on

General Data Protection Regulation

  • GDPR was enforced in May 2018, replacing the older Data Protection Directive 95/96/ec. It aims to create more consistent protection of information on the internet across the European Union. This includes both personal and consumer data. The previous regulation was not adequate due to the increasing use of the internet.
  • Website Considerations:
  • - Data Collection: Reduce the amount of data collected from the users e.g email only for "contact us" submissions
  • - Privacy Notice: Clear declaration of privacy policy e.g state that data will not be shared without consent
  • - Data Breach: Ensure data is secure. Check with hosting provider what systems/software are inplace to combat this
  • - Cookies/Plugins: Ensure GDPR compliant. Gain consent if cookies track information
